Child Safety Standards

Dapster is a dating app intended exclusively for adults aged 18 and older. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and take child safety seriously. This page outlines our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

1. Age Restriction

Dapster is strictly an 18+ platform. Users must confirm they are 18 or older before accessing the app. Any user found to be under 18 will have their account permanently removed.

2. Zero Tolerance for CSAM

Dapster has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and child sexual exploitation. Any content that sexualises minors is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to the relevant authorities.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report any concerning content or behaviour directly within the app. All reports are reviewed promptly. Reports involving child safety are treated with the highest priority.

  • In-app report button available on every profile and message
  • Reports are reviewed by our team as quickly as possible
  • Accounts found violating child safety policies are permanently banned

4. Compliance with Authorities

Dapster complies with all applicable child safety laws. We report any confirmed CSAM or child exploitation content to the appropriate regional and national authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where required.

5. Content Moderation

We monitor the platform for policy violations. Users who attempt to use Dapster to contact or exploit minors will be permanently banned and reported to law enforcement.

6. User Responsibility

All users are required to comply with our Terms and Conditions and child safety policies. By using Dapster, users confirm they are 18 or older and agree not to engage in any behaviour that exploits or harms minors.
