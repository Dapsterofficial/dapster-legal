Dapster — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026 · Version 4.0

This Privacy Policy explains how Dapster ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Dapster mobile application and related services (the "Service"). Capitalised terms not defined here have the meaning given in our Terms of Service.

1. About the Service

Dapster is a "reset dating app" — an anonymous, persona-based social platform whose sole purpose is to help adults find, connect, and make friends. Just friends. Dapster is not a soulmate-finder, not a romantic-matchmaking service, and not a marriage service. The Service is open to all eligible adults regardless of relationship status — single, in a relationship, married, or otherwise — provided they use it for friendship-oriented interaction only.

The Service is designed to minimise the collection of identifying information. Each profile on the Service is a self-authored "Persona" that does not represent the real identity, gender, appearance, or relationship status of the User behind it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Sign-in identifier. When you sign in via Google Sign-In, we receive the Google account email address and a stable user identifier issued by Google. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Persona content. Anything you choose to add to your Persona — persona name, self-declared age (date of birth), self-expressed gender or character, photos, bio text, interests, and preferences.
  • Approximate location. When you grant location permission, we collect your device's location to surface nearby Personas. We store only an approximate location; precise GPS coordinates are not shared with other Users — only an approximate distance is displayed.
  • Device & usage data. Device model, operating system version, app version, language, push-notification token, crash logs, and basic usage events used to operate and improve the Service.
  • Communications. Messages and content you exchange with other Personas through the Service. Messages may be retained on our servers for the period necessary to deliver and moderate the Service.
  • Payment metadata. If you purchase a subscription or in-app product, Google Play processes the payment and shares with us only metadata necessary to grant entitlements (e.g. order ID, product ID, purchase status). We do not receive your card or banking details.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and operate your Persona and account.
  • To surface nearby Personas, conversations, and matches.
  • To deliver push notifications you have opted into.
  • To moderate content, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms and Community Standards.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities.

4. Visibility of Your Persona Content

Persona content (persona name, photos, bio, self-expressed gender or character, approximate distance) is shown to other Users of the Service so that they can decide whether to interact with your Persona. You are responsible for the content you choose to publish on your Persona. Do not include real-world personal information you do not wish to be visible to other Users.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Other Users, in the form of your Persona content.
  • Service providers who host and operate the Service on our behalf (e.g. cloud hosting, database, push-notification delivery, content-moderation tools), bound by confidentiality and data-protection terms.
  • Legal authorities where disclosure is required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Dapster or others.
  • Successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to a binding commitment to honour this Policy.

6. Retention

We retain account and Persona data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your Persona, photos, messages, and associated data are deleted from our active systems within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law (for example, for tax, fraud prevention, or legal-hold purposes) or to enforce our Terms.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal data we hold about you, to withdraw consent, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise most of these rights directly through the in-app settings (edit Persona, delete account). For other requests, contact us at the address below.

8. Children

The Service is strictly for adults aged 18 or above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we learn that a User is under 18, we will terminate the account and delete the associated data.

9. Security

We use industry-standard technical and organisational measures, including encryption in transit and access controls, to protect personal data. No internet service can be guaranteed to be 100% secure; you use the Service at your own risk.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in jurisdictions outside your country of residence, including the cloud regions of our service providers. Where required by applicable law, we put appropriate safeguards in place for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the Service. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

12. Contact

For questions or to exercise your rights, contact dapsterofficial@gmail.com.
